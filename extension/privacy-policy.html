UT Schedule Builder — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

UT Schedule Builder is a Chrome extension that helps University of Texas at Austin students generate class schedules. This policy explains how the extension handles your data.

Data We Access

The extension accesses two UT Austin websites using your existing browser session:

  • utdirect.utexas.edu — to fetch course section data (times, professors, rooms) for the current semester's schedule
  • catalog.utexas.edu — to fetch the list of available courses per department (public data, no login required)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your UT EID, password, or session tokens
  • We do not send any data to external servers, analytics services, or third parties
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not use cookies or access your cookies
  • We do not store any personal information

How Data Is Processed

All data processing happens entirely within your browser. Course data fetched from UT Austin is used only to generate schedule combinations and is not persisted beyond the current session. No data ever leaves your device.

Permissions Explained

  • storage — used to save your schedule preferences locally in your browser
  • Host access to utdirect.utexas.edu — required to fetch course schedule data using your logged-in session
  • Host access to catalog.utexas.edu — required to fetch the complete list of courses per department

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
